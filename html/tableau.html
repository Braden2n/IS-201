Profits are strongest in California at a profit of $78,000, and profits are the weakest in Texas at a loss of $25,000. California and New York have substantially higher profits than the next state; Washington.

Profits in California are very seasonal, with fluctuations every month. A large increase in profits from June-August 2014 was followed by a drop in January 2015.

The most profitable product categories in California were Accessories, Binders, Paper, Copiers, and Storage. With the exception of Tables, all categories were profitable in California.

The sales projections for Copiers follow similar seasonality and fluctiations as in past months. It is expected to stay relatively steady; likely around $1,750 in profits in November of 2015.
